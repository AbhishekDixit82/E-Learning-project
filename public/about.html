Our Stories

Tech Vector Volume was launched in the year 2021 by Akshat Khandelwal with the motive “LET ALL STUDENTS OF INDIA GET EDUCATED,” not just in cities but also in rural areas.

Our Mission

Many Students Can’t Afford Learning Resources Due To The High Cost; We Cater To These Students By Providing The Same At No Cost Or At Lower Prices That They Can Access For Their Entire Life.

Our Vision

We At Tech Vector Volume Provide Science & Technology Related Information. Focused On Individual Development, Tech Vector Volume Breaks The Boundaries Of Financial Background By Providing The Best Content At No-Cost Or Budget-Friendly Prices.

Why Choose Us

We Provide In The Domain Of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Ethical Hacking, Coding & Debugging, Cloud Computing, Designing & Development, Cyber Security, Graphic Designing, UI/UX Designing, Video Editing, And Many More At No-Cost Or Budget-Friendly Prices...

Award for Best StartUp in LSM JAIPUR 2022

IIT-Kharagpur's Entrepreneurship Cell recognized us as the Best Start-Up in the Edu-Tech category at LSM JAIPUR 2022 among all the Startups in Rajasthan.

You’re learning from the best.

We now have 5+ IITians who are continually educating, nurturing, and encouraging students by conducting Webinars, Seminars, Conferences, Expert Talks, and many more...

Collaborated with 200+ Influencers

We've onboarded over 200+ Influencers & Creators, 100+ Artists, 500+ Coders & Developers, and 15,000+ Young Brains on our platform thus far.

We encourage students to INNOVATE

Entrepreneurship, Innovation, Science & Technology, Robotics, Hackathons, Coding Contests, Interviews, and Placements are all areas in which we help students improve their talents.
